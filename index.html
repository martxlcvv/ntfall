ABOUT NIGHTFALL CURSE

Night Fall Curse is a horror-survival runner game inspired by Philippine folklore. Face terrifying mythological enemies while uncovering the dark story hidden within.

CHARACTERS AND ENEMIES

THE RUNNER

The main character, a survivor running through cursed lands to escape folklore monsters.

ASWANG

A shapeshifting ghoul that hunts during the night.

MANANANGGAL

A terrifying creature that splits its body in half to hunt.

DWARF

A sinister dwarf from folklore, lurking in the dark and leading travelers astray.

SIGBIN

An evil spirit that takes the form of a baby to lure victims.

WHITE LADY

A vengeful ghost often seen wandering lonely roads.

GAMEPLAY MECHANICS

CONTROLS (Mobile & Laptop)

Jump, Slide, Left/Right movement, Crouch, Sprint

Obstacles

Barricades, Creepy faces, Stones, Ghosts, Bats

COLLECTIBLES
  • Sprint (speed boost)
  • Heart Life (extra life/restore health)
  • Toxin (reduces health)
  • Freeze (stops enemies/slow motion)

LEVELS AND ENVIRONMENT

GUBAT NG DILIM (Quezon City)

A haunted forest where shadows whisper and danger lurks behind every tree.

CAPIZ

A cursed province, home to terrifying folklore creatures waiting in the dark.

KAYBIANG TUNNEL

A long, eerie tunnel where you can never tell what’s real and what’s an illusion.
